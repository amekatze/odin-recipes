Roasted Chicken and Veggies with Yoghurt Dip

Description

Oven roasted veggies and chicken. Served with rice and yoghurt dip.

Ingredients

Roast

  • Chicken
  • Potatoes
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Onions
  • All Seasoning
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Dip

  • Yoghurt
  • Mayonnaise
  • Harissa Paste
  • Garlic
  • Chili Powder

Step by step

  1. Prepare the dip severals hours in advance.
  2. Just mix ingredients together as listed above and store in refridgerator.
  3. Chop veggies, cut chicken. Set in a bowl.
  4. Add spices and olive oil, mix well.
  5. Roast at 200 degrees for up to an hour.
