Tomato Curry

Description

Rich and tasty tomato based curry.

Ingredients

Roast

  • Chicken
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Cans of cut Tomato
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • Ginger paste
  • Garam Masala
  • Coriander Powder
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 400ml Coconut Milk

Step by step

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add the chicken and onions and fry until golden.
  3. Add about 300ml of water, tomato cans and spices.
  4. Simmer on low heat for 40min.
  5. Add crushed garlic, ginger paste and coconut milk.
  6. Cook for another 10min.
  7. Serve with rice.
